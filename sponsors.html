Sponsors


We’re trying to make this event as accessible as possible in order to promote the best practices and technologies that will help drive a world-class DevOps culture here in South Africa. For this to happen, we need some help. We aren’t offering packages to sponsors but would rather engage each sponsor personally to arrange something beneficial for both parties. If you or your company are in a position to help with funding, equipment, speakers, or anything else, please get in touch with us.
