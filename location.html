Location

Cape Town, South Africa

What to do here

A top tourist destination, there are many things to do and see. The Cape Town tourism department has a great website, capetown.travel with some ideas to set you going in the right direction.

Getting Here

Cape Town is easily accessible from the rest of the country and the world. It has a top-class airport with flights arriving domestically and internationally. To compare the prices of tickets, visit Travelstart.

Airlines flying domestically to Cape Town from within South Africa are: South African Airways, Mango, 1time

Airlines flying internationally to Cape Town include most major international airlines: British Airways, Royal Dutch Airlines, Delta Airways, Lufthansa Airways, Quatar Airways, Emeriates

Transport in Cape Town

South Africa has a good long-distance bus system however, it's recommended to use flying to get around the country due to the time and distance (and better quality of travel). Bus companies include Greyhound, Translux and Intercape. If you'd prefer the backpacker style of getting around the country, you might want to check out Bazbus. Once in Cape Town, you can get around using the relatively new MyCiti bus system, or you can use one of the many taxi companies. If you are going to use taxi's, we recommend Excite Taxis, or Rikki's.
