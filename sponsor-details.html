Sponsorship Details

Sponsorship helps to keep the cost of tickets low as Scaleconf is largely organised by volunteers, low-cost organisations and the help of friends. If none of the options suit you, contact us with your sponsorship ideas!

/bin (Platinum)

R75 000 (9400 USD depending on exchange rate)

Our premier sponsorship package is aimed at companies that would like the focus to be on their brand for the majority of the conference.

  • This package is limited to a maximum of two sponsors
  • Banner on the speaker stage
  • Prominent logo on scaleconf.org homepage
  • Logo and paragraph on scaleconf.org sponsors page

/usr/bin (Gold)

R30 000 (3700 USD depending on exchange rate)

Our second tier sponsorship package is for companies that wish to have a presence at the conference, but not take the spotlight.

  • This package is limited to a maximum of four sponsors
  • Prominent logo on scaleconf.org homepage
  • Logo and paragraph on scaleconf.org sponsors page

/usr/local/bin (Silver)

R15 000 (1800 USD depending on exchange rate)

This sponsorship package is aimed at local companies and startups who wish to make a contribution in return for some minor branding and mentions.

  • This package is limited to ten sponsors
  • Logo and paragraph on scaleconf.org sponsors page

Party Sponsorship

R10 000 - R30 000 (1200-3700 USD depending on exchange rate)

After the first day, the restaurant at the venue will be open until midnight, and offers a great opportunity for a sponsor to provide a good night of entertainment, network, and engage local talent.
