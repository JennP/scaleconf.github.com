What’s it all about?

ScaleConf is a two-day tech conference which brings together local and international experts to share their ideas on tools and methodologies for dealing with the problems of being a highly available service.

The presentations will cover the culture and technologies used to develop and architect strong foundations required to operate web sites and web services at scale.
