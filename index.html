ScaleConf

19-20 January 2012, Cape Town

Scalability, High Availability, and the DevOps movement.

What is it all about?

ScaleConf is a two day tech conference which aims to bring together local and international experts to share their ideas on tools and methodologies used to deal with the problems of being a highly available service.

The presentations will cover the culture and technologies used to develop and architect strong foundations required to operate sites at scale.
