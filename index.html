What is it all about

ScaleConf is a two day tech conference which aims to bring the latest tools and methodologies currently being used at some of the largest websites in the world. There will be talks focused on the culture and technologies used to develop and architect strong foundations required to operate sites at scale. In the limelight will be technologies such as hadoop, puppet, vagrant, jenkins, git and aws. (maybe change to continuous deployment, version control, etc...)
