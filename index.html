ScaleConf

January 18/19 2012, Cape Town

Scaleconf is a web scalability conference about developing and architecting highly scalable websites. International and local experts will be sharing their knowledge and experience, bringing South Africa the latest trending technologies and methodologies.
We are still on the lookout for some speakers to amaze us, and sponsors to help support this incredible event.

Contact us, we're not zombies. »

You should go if...

You build websites. Even if you don't operate at scale, the best practices and lessons learnt will help build the most stable of platforms going forward. Speakers will share their knowledge on creating sites and companies that scale well. A lot of this will focus on using methodologies and technologies that help to reduce the manual work through automation, and improve the efficiency of operations
